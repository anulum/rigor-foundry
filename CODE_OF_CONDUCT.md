# Community Participation Standard

## Purpose

RigorFoundry welcomes rigorous technical disagreement and requires respectful,
evidence-based participation. Access to the project must not depend on age,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience,
nationality, appearance, race, caste, colour, religion, sex characteristics,
sexual identity or orientation, or socio-economic status.

## Expected conduct

- Address the work and its evidence rather than the person.
- State uncertainty, limitations, and conflicts of interest.
- Give specific, reproducible feedback and accept correction when evidence
  changes.
- Protect private reports, credentials, and proprietary repository material.
- Respect maintainers' boundaries, review decisions, and publication gates.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory remarks, sexualised conduct, trolling,
personal attacks, deliberate disclosure of private information, and sustained
disruption are not accepted.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to **protoscience@anulum.li**. Maintainers
will limit disclosure to people needed for a fair review, consider the context
and impact, and document the enforcement decision privately. Responses may
include a correction request, warning, temporary restriction, rejection of a
contribution, or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository spaces, project communications, events, and
public situations where a participant officially represents RigorFoundry.
